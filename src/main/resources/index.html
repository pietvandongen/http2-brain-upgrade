You are looking at a demonstration of HTTP/2.

Its key differences to HTTP/1.x are:

  • Binary format, instead of text.
  • Full multiplexing, instead of ordering and blocking.
  • Just one connection used for parallelism.
  • Header compression, reducing overhead.
  • Allows servers to push their responses proactively.
